WEDDINGS

BRING YOUR DREAMS TO US AND LET US DO THE REST!

Your love story is unique, and your wedding should be too. Perfect for weddings and special functions, our Mansion Hall and Royal BallRoom offers customizable wedding packages designed to suit your individual style and budget. Whether you desire a traditional ceremony or a contemporary celebration with a modern twist, our team will work closely with you to create a wedding experience that exceeds your expectations. Well-equipped with a fully electronic dance floor and modern facilities, Mansion hall can accommodate around 350 guests, however, Royal Ballroom can accommodate up to 150 guests.

CORPORATE EVENTS

BRING YOUR DREAMS TO US AND LET US DO THE REST!

At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, your happiness is our priority. Whether you're hosting a conference, team-building retreat, seminar or corporate banquet, our well-equipped Conference Hall offers the perfect blend of sophistication, comfort, and functionality to ensure the success of your corporate event. Situated at the Timber Trail Heights, Parwanoo, it covers 2095 square feet in area and is an excellent venue for offsite conferences. From high-speed internet access and audiovisual equipment to gourmet catering services and accommodation up to 100 guests, we provide everything you need to ensure a seamless and productive experience for you and your guests.
