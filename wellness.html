REJUVENATE AT MOKSHA

Designed for the guests to disconnect from all the daily chores, our wellness spa is an oasis of pure relaxation. Allow our qualified masseurs to take away all your exhaustion and stress by pampering you with professional spa therapies.

Inclusions
6 single treatment rooms • 3 couple treatment rooms • 2 Ayurvedic treatment rooms
1 full size hamam • 1 Thai massage room • 1 salon • Changing rooms • Fitness centre • Yoga pavilion

The Moksha Spa is for the exclusive use of our resident guests only

AYURVEDIC TREATMENTS

Our specially curated wellness and beauty spa rituals combine select treatments to rejuvenate and relax the body and being. Each Oberoi Experience caters to your specific needs, delivers holistic care and transports you to a state of peace and calm.

The journey begins with long, symmetrical massage strokes delivered with a warm poultice, prepared with Indian herbs and warm therapeutic oil. This is followed by a customized facial to suit your needs. The experience concludes with a classic head massage for the scalp, neck, and shoulders. This experience promotes a profound sense of oneness and well-being.

Therapies Inclusion:

  • Hot Poultice
  • Facial
  • Head massage

OTHER SERVICES

Salon

Indulge in luxury at the salon of Moksha Himalaya Spa and Resort. Our expert stylists offer a range of services, from rejuvenating facials to precision haircuts, ensuring you look and feel your best. Experience personalized care in a serene setting, perfect for relaxation and revitalisation.

Hamam

Experience ultimate relaxation at our Hammam at Moksha Himalaya Spa and Resort. Step into a soothing sanctuary where warm steam and traditional treatments melt away stress, rejuvenate your body, and revitalize your spirit. Enjoy a timeless ritual that leaves you feeling refreshed and renewed in the serene Himalayas.

Fitness Centre

Our Fitness Centre at Moksha Himalaya Spa and Resort offers state-of-the-art equipment and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're maintaining your routine or starting fresh, enjoy a workout with stunning views of the Himalayas. Join us to stay fit, feel great, and embrace the serene surroundings.

Infinity Pool

Dive into luxury at Moksha Himalaya Spa and Resort's infinity pool. Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains as you swim in serenity. Whether you're taking a leisurely dip or soaking in the sun, our infinity pool offers the perfect blend of relaxation and natural beauty.
