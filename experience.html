EXPERIENCES AT MOKSHA

At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, every moment is an opportunity to reconnect with yourself and the natural world, to find peace, balance, and harmony. We excel in offering an unparalleled retreat for those seeking tranquility, rejuvenation, and holistic wellness. We are the only spa resort nearby Chandigarh set against the backdrop of majestic snow-capped peaks and lush green valleys, this luxurious resort is a sanctuary for the mind, body, and soul. Contact us today to book your stay and embark on a journey of rejuvenation at Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, Himachal Pradesh.

Cable Car

We cordially invite you to experience a thrilling cable car ride at Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort to enhance your mountain retreat. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a new perspective on the world around you, our cable cars promise an unforgettable journey through the heart of the Himalayas. Embark on a scenic journey as you glide effortlessly through the sky in our comfortable cable cars. Enjoy panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and winding rivers below, as our experienced operators guide you on an unforgettable adventure.


Please Note: The Cable Car can be boarded from the base at Timber Trail Resort from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Luxurious Spa

Immerse yourself in a world of relaxation and indulgence at our rejuvenating spa. With a range of rejuvenating treatments inspired by ancient Ayurvedic practices and modern wellness techniques, our skilled therapists will pamper you from head to toe, leaving you feeling refreshed, revitalised, and renewed. Unwind amidst the serenity of nature as you soak in the panoramic views of the Himalayas from our sprawling gardens, tranquil meditation spaces, and scenic outdoor lounges

Infinity Pool

Within the tranquil and unparalleled world of Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort's infinity pool, each moment is an invitation to relax, revitalise, and restore your relationship with the surrounding natural beauty. Our infinity pool's glistening clear waters will make you feel as though you're floating off the edge of the planet. Because of this, our infinity pool is only available to hotel visitors, guaranteeing a calm and cosy setting where you can fully decompress and disconnect from the outside world. Every visitor to Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, in our opinion, should be able to enjoy the height of exclusivity and elegance.

Play Area for Kids

At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, we understand that family vacations are all about creating unforgettable memories together. That's why we're thrilled to offer our youngest guests an exciting and engaging play area that promises endless fun and adventure. Let your little ones unleash their inner adventurer in our outdoor play area, complete with swings, slides, and climbing frames. Watch as they explore the natural beauty of the Himalayas while climbing to new heights and sliding down to laughter-filled landings.

Bon-fire

There's something truly magical about gathering around a crackling bonfire under the starlit sky, surrounded by the majestic beauty of the Himalayas. At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, we invite you to immerse yourself in the warmth and camaraderie of our unforgettable bonfire experiences. Whether you're seeking a cosy evening with loved ones or a lively gathering with fellow travellers, our bonfire events promise to ignite your senses and create memories that last a lifetime.

Candlelit Dinners

We invite you to indulge in the romance of a candlelit dinner and create moments that you'll treasure forever. At Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort, we believe in creating unforgettable moments of romance and intimacy amidst the serene beauty of the Himalayas. Our candlelit dinners offer the perfect opportunity for couples to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in a world of love, luxury, and culinary delights. Contact us today to reserve your private dining experience and embark on a journey of love and luxury amidst the enchanting beauty of the Himalayas.

Nearby Attractions

Situated amidst the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas, Moksha Himalaya Spa Resort offers not only a tranquil retreat but also convenient access to some of the region's most captivating attractions. We invite you to explore the beauty and charm of Himachal Pradesh with ease, thanks to our convenient location near these captivating attractions. Whether you're seeking adventure, cultural immersion, or simply a peaceful retreat amidst nature, our resort serves as the perfect starting point for your Himalayan adventure.
